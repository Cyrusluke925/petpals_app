Pet Pals!

About

. . . a social media site for pets!

Pet Pals is a social media oriented, full-stack web application that connects pet owners by means of sharing images of their pets. Using Django, Python, Javascript, jQuery, HTML, and CSS, our goal is to create an application that allows users to sign-up, create a profile for their pet, post pictures of their pets, and interact with other users through likes and comments. Users will be able to follow other pets, as well as explore randomly generated posts of pets that they are not yet following.
