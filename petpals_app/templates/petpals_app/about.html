Pet Pals!

About

. . . a social media site for pets!

Pet Pals is a social media app for pet parents. Our goal is to create an application that allows users to sign-up, create a profile for their pet, post pictures of their pets, and interact with other users through likes and comments. Users will be able to follow other pets, as well as explore randomly generated posts of pets that they are not yet following.

We were founded by a group of students in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive who are passionate about their pets and wanted to find a place to connect with other pet parents.
