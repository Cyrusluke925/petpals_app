About Pet Pals

If you have a passion for pets, Pet Pals is the social media platform you've been waiting for! Pet Pals connects passionate pet parents by interacting on eachothers posts by sharing, liking, and commenting on user posts.

About Our Creators

We were founded by three web development students at General Assembly, Natalie, Luke, and Andrea. They have a passion for pets and wanted to create a platform to connect pet owners.

Natalie loves animals, especially Moo - her formerly feral feline from Carlsbad, New Mexico. Luke was raised in the Bay Area, Luke's interest in web development sparked when he decided to join a prepatory class in JavaScript. He enjoys long walks on the beach, music and Netflix. Andrea relocated to San Francisco from a quaint town in Connecticut. She loves animals, has been a vegetarian for seven years, and adores her formerly stray Norwiegen Forest Cat, Ella and Australian Shepard, Woofie.
